Technology Overview

We use TensorFlow and Teachable Machine to provide precise skin analysis. The model is trained with thousands of skin condition images to ensure accuracy.

Step-by-Step Process

  • Step 1: Upload or take a photo of your skin.
  • Step 2: Our AI model analyzes the image and identifies skin conditions.
  • Step 3: You receive personalized skincare advice based on the analysis.

How the Model is Trained

Our model is trained on a diverse dataset of skin conditions to ensure accuracy in predicting acne, wrinkles, and other skin issues.
